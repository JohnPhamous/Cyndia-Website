Summer Science Program 2012

Asteroid Orbit Determination

I feel like it is part of my duty as an alum of this summer program to get up on a soapbox and preach about why it was a life-changing experience for me. Even now, looking back several years later, I am amazed by the sheer amount of talent and brains in a lot of the students and teaching staff. If you are a high school student interested in science and/or engineering, I highly recommend you check it out. If not, I'm sure any support you can provide would be much appreciated to keep this opportunity affordable to students.

Things we did/learned about ranged among:

  • orbital dynamics
  • basic python
  • iterative solvers
  • image processing
  • site visits (JPL!!)
  • guest lectures
  • fun field trips (such as to the Getty)
  • biweekly beach trips (where I discovered my love of beach volleyball)
