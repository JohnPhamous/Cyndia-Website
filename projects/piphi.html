Pi Beta Phi Recruitment Decor

Fall 2016

Ever since moving into the Pi Phi house in my junior year, I've been very involved in the chapter. During recruitment in particular, I would always come home and see lots of people crafting on the first floor and hanging out before school started. I got close to two of my best friends during this time. Since I love crafting and the act of cutting things up and putting them back together, I decided that I would spend my senior fall setting up room decor to blow away all of the potential new members...

The theme?

Midnight in Paris

The buildings were modelled off of a silhouette of the Paris skyline. They were cut from various pieces of cardboard and had to be reinforced using PVC in order to preserve its crisp shape.


The stars were folded from a laser cut perforation template.

I should also send out a shout-out to my other favorite day of decorations. Hope this inspires some other great designs for any chapters browsing the interwebs.
