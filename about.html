A little bit about me...

Engineering is not just major, it's my hobby. I've loved designing and building things ever since I joined my high school's FIRST Robotics team way back when. But more than that, I love the environment of people who build things for fun. Everyone I've met is always looking to have a good time and make some cool sh!t. The most important factor to me as I graduate and move on is getting access to crazy cool projects and being able to work in a hands-on, rapid prototyping lab environment. I find that the constant free flow of information in an open space is the best way to learn and grow, and most importantly to have a fun time with colleagues.


In my limited free time, I like going on all kinds of adventures. One weekend this summer, a couple friends and I overnighted a drive up to Yosemite, stopping to watch the Perseid meteor shower. At home, I love going out to play laser tag with friends. Want to tackle an escape room? Don't forget to hit me up! And even though I'm a noob at volleyball, I LOVE to play sand volleyball. And I love puppies, especially the crazy ones.
